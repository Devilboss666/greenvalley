Nearby Places and Things to do

Lonavala Ghat

Experience the breathtaking Lonavala Ghat near Karjat,offering winding roads amidst lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and panoramic vistas. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike,the ghat promises an exhilarating journey through the Western Ghats' scenic beauty.

kondana caves

Discover the ancient marvels of Kondana Caves near Karjat, Maharashtra, featuring intricately carved Buddhist rock-cut caves dating back to the 1st century BCE. Immerse yourself in history amidst serene surroundings, making it an ideal destination for cultural exploration and trekking enthusiasts alike.

River Rafting Karjat

Experience the thrill of river rafting in Karjat, Maharashtra, as you navigate through the rapids of the Kundalika River, one of the fastest flowing rivers in the region. With expert guides and stunning natural scenery, Karjat offers an adrenaline-pumping adventure for both beginners and experienced rafters amidst picturesque surroundings.

Visit bhimashankar

Embark on a spiritual journey to Bhimashankar Temple near Karjat, Maharashtra, nestled amidst the lush Sahyadri hills. Explore ancient architecture and tranquil surroundings, and trek through scenic trails, immersing yourself in the natural beauty and religious significance of the region.
